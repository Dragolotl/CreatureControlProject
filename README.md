# Critter Control!

For our project, three of the patterns we will be using are factory pattern, decorator pattern, and strategy pattern. <br><br>
Factory Pattern will be used for creating our different Creatures whenever a new Creature needs to be made. 
<br>

Decorator Pattern will be used for customizing our Creatures when they receive different hats that increase their stats!   <br>

Finally, Strategy Pattern will be used for coding our Creatures dynamic behavior, as the behavior of the Creatures will change when they reach higher levels. <br>

## Accessories
Players will be awarded various accessories as they play. Critters can wear these accessories, and some even provide helpful stat bonuses.

Critters can only wear one accessory at a time and each accessory can only be worn by one critter at a time.
